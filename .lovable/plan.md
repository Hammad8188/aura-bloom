

# NOIR ESSENCE - Premium Perfume E-Commerce Platform

A complete luxury perfume e-commerce website with an elegant customer experience and comprehensive admin dashboard.

---

## Brand Identity

**Name:** NOIR ESSENCE  
**Tagline:** "Where Luxury Meets Fragrance"  
**Color Palette:** Deep black, champagne gold (#D4AF37), soft cream (#F5F5DC), white  
**Typography:** Elegant serif headings (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif body (Inter)

---

## Part 1: Customer-Facing Website

### Homepage
- Full-screen hero with animated perfume bottle imagery and brand tagline
- Featured collections carousel (Best Sellers, New Arrivals, Limited Edition)
- Product showcase grid with 8 featured perfumes and hover effects
- Brand story section with elegant visuals
- Customer testimonials slider with 5-star ratings
- Newsletter subscription form
- Instagram-style image gallery
- Luxury footer with quick links and social media

### Shop/Collection Page
- Grid/List view toggle with smooth transitions
- Advanced filtering sidebar:
  - Gender (Men, Women, Unisex)
  - Fragrance family (Floral, Woody, Oriental, Fresh, Citrus, Oud)
  - Price range slider
  - Size options (30ml, 50ml, 100ml)
  - Occasion filters
- Sort options (Price, Popularity, Newest, Rating)
- Search with autocomplete suggestions
- Product cards with quick-view, wishlist, and add-to-cart
- Pagination with elegant styling

### Product Detail Page
- Image gallery with zoom and thumbnails (4-6 images per product)
- Size selector with pricing variations
- Quantity selector with stock indicator
- Visual fragrance pyramid (Top, Heart, Base notes)
- Longevity and sillage indicators
- Expandable description sections
- "Add to Cart" and "Buy Now" buttons
- Related products and "Customers Also Bought" sections
- Reviews section with star ratings

### Shopping Cart
- Slide-out cart drawer + dedicated cart page
- Product cards with image, details, quantity controls
- Remove and save-for-later options
- Subtotal and delivery calculation
- Discount code input
- Continue shopping and checkout buttons
- Empty cart state with shop redirect

### Checkout Flow
- Step-by-step progress indicator (Info → Shipping → Payment)
- Customer information form with validation
- Delivery options (Standard/Express)
- Payment selection:
  - Cash on Delivery
  - Credit Card (Stripe-ready UI)
- Order summary sidebar
- Order confirmation page with order number

### Additional Pages
- **About Us:** Brand story timeline, mission/vision, quality promises
- **Contact:** Form, WhatsApp button, social links, contact details
- **Wishlist:** Saved products with add-to-cart option
- **My Account:** Profile info and order history
- **Track Order:** Order status lookup
- **FAQ:** Accordion-style common questions
- **Policies:** Shipping, Returns, Privacy pages

## Technical Features

- **Responsive Design:** Mobile-first approach for all screen sizes
- **Smooth Animations:** Page transitions, hover effects, loading states
- **Form Validation:** Client-side validation on all forms
- **Toast Notifications:** Success/error feedback throughout
- **Loading States:** Skeleton loaders and spinners
- **SEO-Friendly:** Proper meta tags and semantic HTML
- **Accessibility:** ARIA labels, keyboard navigation support
